Fredrik chats to [Sergès Goma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/devserges/?locale=en)

All the great versions of a talk - you never know what's going to happen.

The pull between protecting your user and finishing your tickets.

Making (good) ethical decisions business decisions instead can suddenly get them budget  and priority. Laws are good at creating such pressures sometimes.

Toward the end, Sergès gets Fredrik to make some predictions about the AI bubble.
